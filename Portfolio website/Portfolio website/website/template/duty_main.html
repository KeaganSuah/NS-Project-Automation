Automated Duty Roster


Instructions

In the military, every serviceman have to perform their operation duty. This project helps to organise and plan out the servicemans duties and ensure it is fair based on the algorithm. The serviceman will first begain their submission of their preferred and unavailable dates. On the given cut of date, superiors will use step 3 to view all of the submissions, and choose wether to accept or reject. Once all the request have been accepted or rejected, superiors will generate out the duty roster by simplying clicking the button. The system follows the algorithm in order to ensure an unbiased and fair duty roster. Superiors can hen download the duty roster and forward to the rest. In the scenerio where a duty personnel would like to make adjustments, they can let their superiors know and they can do the necessary amendments using step 6.


Incoming / Outgoing Personnel

Main Operation

Step 1: Duty Personnel submit their preferred duty date


Step 2: Duty Personnel submit their unavailable dates


Step 3: Superiors Accept or Reject the individuals unavailable dates


Step 4: Superiors press this to Generate the Duty Roster.

Superiors have to accepted or rejected all submission before generating the roster.


Step 5: Once generated, superiors download the duty roster and forward it to the duty personnel


Step 6: Any amendments made between duty personnel, for example exchange of duties, can be done by superiors
