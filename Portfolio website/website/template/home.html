Digital Attendance Taking


Instructions

Purpose

This project helps to gather the attendance more efficiently through the comfort of the serviceman's phone.

Process

Individuals will first begin their submission of reasons for not being present during roll call. If the individuals are present for the next day roll call, they are not required to do anything.

Superiors proceed to mark their branch attendances, they can also view their entire branch attendance after roll call is done. The last step is for senior management to export their entire organisation attendance for the day.
